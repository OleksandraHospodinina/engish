Why us

In addition to regular exercises, we offer students an interactive platform to watch videos, read books, study from the best and internationally recognized textbooks, do homework and be creative. This allows you to make progress in your studies much faster.

80% practice and 20% theory. The purpose of the methodology is the development of practical and functional speech. We focus on real life situations, such as household affairs, everyday life, hobbies, sports, booking tickets at a hotel or cinema, ordering by phone.

Every week, students will be able to attend Speaking Club with native speakers for speaking practice. Students will be able to additionally practice articulation, pronunciation of complex sounds and speaking in general.

Current topics of the lessons: as part of the study, students discuss current topics: generational problems, globalization, slang, cryptocurrency. BBC and CNN films involving native speakers contribute to the expansion of vocabulary and film vocabulary in modern terms.
