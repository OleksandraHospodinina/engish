FORMATS OF CLASSES

For Kids

For Adults

Regular Lessons

With the help of the teacher, students learn general rules and practices of English for comprehensive development of knowledge, skills, and abilities.

● Vocabulary development

● Grammar practices

● Conversations about daily life

● Writing messages

● Reading and listening

● Homework analysis

● Creating drawings using communication only in English

Integrated Lessons

Integrated lessons expand knowledge in other subjects through the use of English.

● Learning botany, biology, politics, economics in English.

● Watching and translating popular TV shows and movies to improve listening skills.

● Expanding vocabulary by learning words from an interesting field for the child.

Project Tasks

Project tasks improve student's creative thinking and allow them to reveal their creative potential.

● Students are invited to create their own product in a shop, banknote, etc.

● Preparation and conducting of product presentations.

● Modeling discussions on a business project.

Content Lessons

Discussing interesting and trending content.

● Practicing pronunciation of American and British accents

● Analyzing YouTube videos, interviews, movies, TV shows, series, news from BBC and CNN.

● Listening and analyzing complex sounds.

Practice Lesson

Practice real-life situations and dialogs that will be useful in life.

● How to order food in a cafe or restaurant.

● How to book a room in a hotel.

● How to ask for directions in an unfamiliar city.

English for Business

English for business is for those interested in IT, international trade, commerce, and finance.

● Students practice business meetings, presentations, negotiations, expand their vocabulary and analyze articles on business topics.

● As homework, we suggest listening to podcasts on marketing, economics, business, and analyzing publications from "The Economist" and "The Wall Street Journal".
